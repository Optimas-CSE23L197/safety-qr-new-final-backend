Backend Profile — Emergency Tips Panel
EMERGENCY RESPONSE GUIDE
Displayed to first responder on QR scan
🩸
Bleeding / Injury

Apply firm pressure with clean cloth. Do not remove embedded objects. Keep the student still and calm.

💊
Allergic Reaction / Medical

Check allergy info above. If anaphylaxis suspected — administer epinephrine if available and call 108 immediately.

🫀
Unconscious / Not Breathing

Begin CPR: 30 chest compressions + 2 rescue breaths. Continue until ambulance arrives. Do not leave the student alone.

🔥
Burns / Chemical Exposure

Flush with cool running water for 10+ mins. Do not apply ice, butter, or creams. Remove contaminated clothing carefully.

🧠
Seizure / Convulsion

Do not restrain. Protect head, clear area. Time the seizure. Roll to recovery position after it stops. Call 108.

Emergency Helpline: 112  |  Ambulance: 108
